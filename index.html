Child Rights NGO in India

Child Rights and You is an agent of change and with your involvement ensures the fundamental rights of underprivileged children in India are protected and honoured. Our website is an open book of our work.

Be a CRY volunteer

Our dedicated volunteer teams working in Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi and Kolkata they have been campaigning, working in networks with local communities to address issues affecting children and their families.

Vision and Mission

A happy, healthy and creative child whose rights are protected and honoured in a society that is built on respect for dignity, justice and equity for all.

How we ensure lasting change

Our experience over three decades of working for children has taught us that lasting change is only possible when we look at the bigger picture.
